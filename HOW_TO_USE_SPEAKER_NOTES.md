# How to Use Speaker Notes for Your Presentation

## üé§ What Are Speaker Notes?

Speaker notes are your **personal reference guide** for the presentation. They're NOT shown to the audience‚Äîthey're for YOU to glance at while presenting.

## üì± How to Add Them (Platform-Specific)

### **If Using Google Slides:**
1. Open your presentation
2. Click **View** ‚Üí **Show speaker notes** (bottom of screen)
3. A text box appears below each slide
4. Copy/paste the relevant notes from `SPEAKER_NOTES_QUICK_REF.md`
5. During presentation: Use **Presenter View** to see notes on your screen while audience sees slides

### **If Using PowerPoint:**
1. Open your presentation
2. Click **View** ‚Üí **Notes Page**
3. Type/paste notes in the notes section below each slide
4. During presentation: Use **Presenter View** (Alt+F5 on Windows, Cmd+Return on Mac)

### **If Using Keynote (Mac):**
1. Open your presentation
2. Click **View** ‚Üí **Show Presenter Notes**
3. Type/paste notes in the Presenter Notes panel
4. During presentation: Use **Play** ‚Üí **Play Slideshow** (notes show on your screen only)

### **If Presenting from PDF:**
Print the `SPEAKER_NOTES_QUICK_REF.md` file and keep it beside you as physical reference.

## üí° Pro Tips

### **During Your Presentation:**
1. **Glance, don't read** - Notes are triggers, not scripts
2. **Pause after key numbers** - Let "25.5x ROI" sink in
3. **Make eye contact** - Look at notes between slides, not during
4. **Time yourself** - Each slide should be ~1-1.5 minutes

### **Practice Run:**
1. Go through presentation once with notes visible
2. Second run: Try to present with just 1-2 glances at notes per slide
3. Third run: See if you can do it without notes (but keep them as backup)

### **What NOT to Do:**
- ‚ùå Don't read notes word-for-word (sounds robotic)
- ‚ùå Don't look down at notes while mid-sentence (breaks flow)
- ‚ùå Don't put too much text in notes (keep it short triggers)

## üì± Digital vs. Physical Notes

### **Option 1: Digital (Recommended for Zoom/Virtual)**
- Use presenter view on laptop
- Notes visible to you, slides visible to audience
- Can copy/paste from `SPEAKER_NOTES_QUICK_REF.md`

### **Option 2: Physical (Good for In-Person)**
- Print `SPEAKER_NOTES_QUICK_REF.md` 
- Keep beside you as reference cards
- Highlight key phrases in yellow
- Number pages to match slide order

### **Option 3: Hybrid (Best of Both)**
- Digital notes in presenter view
- Physical 1-page "cheat sheet" with just power phrases
- Glance at physical sheet for confidence boosts
